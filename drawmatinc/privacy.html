Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Drawmatinc! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and the Brush Stroke Patterns Drawing Tool. As a solo developer, I’m committed to keeping your experience simple, safe, and private. This policy was last updated on March 23, 2025.

Please read this policy carefully. By using Drawmatinc, you agree to its terms. If you have questions, feel free to reach out via the Contact page.

Information We Collect

The Brush Stroke Patterns Drawing Tool is designed to run entirely in your browser, meaning I don’t collect or store personal data on any server. Here’s what you should know:

  • No Personal Data Collected: The tool doesn’t require accounts, logins, or personal details like your name, email, or location.
  • Local Processing: All drawing, image uploads (e.g., for color sampling or backgrounds), and settings are processed locally on your device. Nothing is sent to me or a server.
  • Usage Data: I don’t track how you use the tool (e.g., brush choices, canvas actions) unless you explicitly share feedback or artwork via the Gallery or Contact pages.

How We Use Your Information

Since I don’t collect personal data, there’s no information to use, share, or sell. The tool’s purpose is to let you create art, and all data stays with you:

  • Your Artwork: When you save your drawing as a PNG, it’s stored on your device. I don’t access it.
  • Uploaded Images: If you upload images for backgrounds or color sampling, they remain in your browser’s memory during your session and are discarded when you close the tab (unless you remove them earlier).

Cookies and Tracking

Drawmatinc doesn’t use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party tracking scripts at this time. Your visit to the site is as private as your device allows:

  • No Cookies: I don’t set cookies to track your behavior.
  • Third-Party Services: The tool doesn’t integrate with external services that might collect data (e.g., Google Analytics).

Note: Your browser or device may store temporary data (e.g., cache) to improve performance, but that’s outside my control.

Data Security

Since no data is sent to me, security focuses on your local environment:

  • Client-Side Safety: The tool uses standard web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) with no known vulnerabilities as of March 23, 2025.
  • Your Responsibility: Keep your device secure (e.g., updated browser, antivirus) to protect your artwork and uploaded files.

Third-Party Links

The Drawmatinc website may include links to other pages (e.g., Gallery, Contact). This Privacy Policy applies only to Drawmatinc itself:

  • External Sites: If future updates link to third-party sites, their privacy practices will differ, and I’m not responsible for them.

Children’s Privacy

The Brush Stroke Patterns Drawing Tool is suitable for all ages, but it’s not specifically targeted at children under 13. I don’t knowingly collect data from anyone, including minors. If you’re a parent and have concerns, please contact me via the Contact page.
