Drawmatinc Brush Stroke Patterns Drawing Tool Tutorial

Introduction

The Drawmatinc Drawing Tool is a powerful web-based application that allows artists to create stunning digital artwork with features like custom brushes, symmetry, and video recording. Whether you're a hobbyist or a professional artist, this tool offers unique opportunities to monetize your creations. In this article, we’ll explore five practical ways to earn money using Drawmatinc, from selling artwork to teaching others how to use the tool.

1. Sell Your Artwork on Online Marketplaces

Drawmatinc’s high-resolution canvas (up to 4K) and custom brush features make it ideal for creating artwork that can be sold on platforms like Etsy, Saatchi Art, or Redbubble. You can create original pieces, prints, or designs for merchandise like t-shirts and mugs.

  • Create Unique Designs: Use Drawmatinc’s symmetry and spiral modes to craft intricate patterns or mandalas, which are popular on art marketplaces.
  • Export High-Quality Images: Save your artwork as PNG files using the tool’s “Save” button for professional-quality prints.
  • List on Marketplaces: Set up a shop on Etsy or Redbubble, upload your designs, and promote them via social media.

Tip: Use the custom brush feature to upload your own patterns, creating distinctive artwork that stands out in crowded marketplaces.

2. Offer Custom Commissions

Drawmatinc’s versatile brush options, including watercolor and curly wings, allow you to create personalized artwork for clients. Platforms like Fiverr and Upwork are great places to offer commission services.

  • Build a Portfolio: Create a variety of pieces in Drawmatinc to showcase your style, such as portraits or logos.
  • Advertise Services: List your drawing services on Fiverr, specifying that you use Drawmatinc for unique effects.
  • Engage Clients: Use the tool’s color sampling feature to match client-specified color schemes for tailored results.

Tip: Record your drawing process using Drawmatinc’s MP4 export feature and share timelapse videos with clients to demonstrate your process.

3. Create and Sell Digital Products

Drawmatinc’s vector-like precision and pattern scaling make it perfect for designing digital products like clipart, fonts, or social media templates. These can be sold on platforms like Creative Market or Gumroad.

  • Design Clipart: Use the custom brush to create repeatable patterns for clipart sets, ideal for scrapbooking or graphic design.
  • Vectorize Artwork: Export your drawings and use software like Adobe Illustrator to convert them into scalable vectors.
  • Package and Sell: Bundle your designs into sets and sell them on Creative Market with clear licensing terms.

Tip: Experiment with the tool’s watercolor spread settings to create organic textures for digital backgrounds.

4. Monetize Process Videos on YouTube or Patreon

Drawmatinc’s ability to record your drawing process as an MP4 opens up opportunities to create engaging content for YouTube, Patreon, or TikTok. Art process videos are highly popular and can generate income through ads or subscriptions.

  • Record Your Work: Use the “Save MP4” feature to capture your drawing sessions in 720p or 1080p.
  • Edit and Upload: Edit the video with free software like DaVinci Resolve, adding music or commentary, and upload to YouTube.
  • Monetize: Enable YouTube ads or offer exclusive content on Patreon for supporters, such as custom brush tutorials.

Tip: Create a series of videos showcasing Drawmatinc’s unique features, like spiral mode, to attract viewers interested in digital art tools.

5. Teach Drawmatinc Workshops or Tutorials

If you’re proficient with Drawmatinc, you can teach others how to use it through online courses or workshops on platforms like Skillshare or Udemy. This is a great way to share your expertise and earn passive income.

  • Plan Your Course: Create a curriculum covering Drawmatinc’s features, such as custom brushes, symmetry, and recording.
  • Record Tutorials: Use screen recording software to demonstrate techniques, exporting examples as MP4s from Drawmatinc.
  • Publish and Promote: Upload your course to Skillshare and promote it on social media to attract students.

Tip: Offer a free introductory video on YouTube to drive interest in your paid course, highlighting Drawmatinc’s ease of use.

The Drawmatinc Drawing Tool is more than just a creative outlet—it’s a gateway to earning money through your art. By leveraging its unique features like custom brushes, high-resolution exports, and video recording, you can sell artwork, offer commissions, create digital products, monetize videos, or teach others. Start by building a portfolio with Drawmatinc, experimenting with its tools, and exploring platforms like Etsy, Fiverr, or YouTube. With dedication and creativity, your Drawmatinc creations can become a profitable side hustle or full-time career.
